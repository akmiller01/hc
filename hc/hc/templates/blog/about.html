About Us

This is what we do.

Sarah Braun is a freelance writer and Alex Miller is a humanitarian web developer. They co-founded Honestly Curated in May 2015 after realizing the direction that fast fashion is taking the world. Honestly Curated is a website where fashion and ethics meet. We do not subscribe to only one form of ethical fashion, but support all that promote sustainability and change in the fashion industry. Simply put, everything that's honest!


Alex and Sarah are both also hobbyist photographers. All of the photos you see on our website have been taken by us and are owned by us unless otherwise specified.

To collaborate with us, contribute to the site, or just say hi, email us at honestlycurated@gmail.com

If you've enjoyed our website, please consider donating to help keep it alive.
